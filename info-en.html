1. How to get there

By plane

The closest airport is Bratislava airport, but it is also possible to land in Budapest or Vienna. From there, you can either rent a car (preferred option), or take a train/bus to Brezno. There is a flixbus running from Bratislava, Vienna or Budapest to Banska Bystrica. You can then take a local bus to Brezno.

By bus/ train

You can take a bus (flixbus) or a train to Banska Bystrica from all major cities around and then a local bus to Brezno.

Please, let us know your plans and we will help you get to your accommodation once you know your exact arrival time to Brezno.
